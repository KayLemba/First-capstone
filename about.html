The Burning Circle Flight Academy

The Burining Circle Flight Academy airshow is one of Zambia's first airshows to be hosted by a privately owned aviation institution. The whole essence of this event is to connect aerospace industry proffessionals across Africa and the world at Large. This is also a great opportunity for youths interested in aviation and becoming Pilots to learn from the best Pilots and know what it takes to become a world class pilot.

Please contact us per email for any further questions.
